Frequently asked questions

What is UpsideFinance?

UpsideFinance is Algorand's first DeFi linked lossless lottery platform. The app encourages users to save by rewarding winners with aggregated interest. As this is a savings platform, users face no risk of losing their deposits.

How are the rewards generated?

Rewards are generated via lending platforms such as AlgoFi that provide a variable interest rate on deposits. The accumulated interest on the pooled deposit acts as a reward for the lossless lottery participants.

Is UpsideFinance decentralised?

At this point, we are using a semi-centralised approach. Users' deposits are transferred from the smart contract to our private wallet, and then supplied to Algofi using their SDK. We understand it's not an ideal approach, but all the transfers are traceable and transparent on-chain. We'll employ a fully decentralised approach once the consensus upgrade is available.

Why can't I deposit/withdraw/claim reward?

We're operating with some restrictions on the app functionality. You can deposit only once in the lossless lottery. Withdrawing and claiming rewards is possible only after the lottery ends and funds are reclaimed from the DeFi protocol.

What are my odds of winning?

Winning odds are directly proportional to your share of deposit in the lossless lottery pool. Later versions of the app will allow users to deposit and leverage their UpsideFinance rewards (rUFt) tokens to increase their winning odds.
