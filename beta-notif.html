Instructions

  1. This app requires ALGO on testnet.
    To get ALGO on testnet, visit Algorand testnet dispenser
  2. Participants need a MyAlgo wallet to deposit
  3. Wait for a few seconds after clicking "Confirm"
  4. After depositing, make sure to sign the TWO transactions. This may take a few seconds
  5. The deposit modal will close on its own after the transactions are successful
  6. Lottery participants can deposit once before the deadline and withdraw only afterwards
  7. One winner is randomly selected at the end of the lottery period
  8. Lottery participants can withdraw their deposit and claim rewards
  9. Refreshing the page might disconnect your wallet
